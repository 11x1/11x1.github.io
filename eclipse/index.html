Features

We offer many features in our script, some of which have never been seen before. Eclipse was created having customization in mind, which is why we offer different indicator designs and an in-depth anti-aim builder.

What is eclipse?

Eclipse is an all-in-one lua script for primordial, neverlose and gamesense. We aim to provide a high quality script for free, to all users. Our community is currently at 500+ members and continues to grow daily. Join our community by clicking our logo above!

Easy to use

We have made every element label very clear and understandable for all of our users. Eclipse's new feature allows users to share their own lua configs (*.eclipse) with other community members. All script's configs can be found in our Discord!
